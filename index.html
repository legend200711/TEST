🤖 AI Community Safety System

Shadow Nexus Social uses a built-in AI-assisted moderation system to help keep the community safe. The AI assists moderators — it does not replace them. Every serious action can be reviewed and appealed.

⚡ Detects rule violations ✅ Tracks repeated issues 📩 Appeal any action

🚫 What Is Not Allowed

These rules apply to posts, stories, and messages.

🚫
No Bullying
Do not target, demean, or persistently harass any individual user on the platform.
🚫
No Harassment
Do not send unwanted, intimidating, or repeated aggressive messages to any user.
🚫
No Threats
Threats of any kind — including implied or indirect threats — will be escalated immediately for review.
🚫
No Hate Speech
Content that discriminates or attacks based on identity, background, or group membership is strictly prohibited.
🚫
No Spam
Do not flood chats, posts, or messages with repetitive, unsolicited, or promotional content.
🚫
No Repeated Disruptive Behavior
Continued rule-breaking after warnings will result in escalating restrictions.

📍 These Rules Apply To

📝 Posts 📖 Stories 💬 Messages

🔄 How the Safety System Works

Rule Broken → AI Detects → Warning Issued → Continued Issue → Action Taken → User Can Appeal → 👑 Founder Reviews → Accept or Deny

📩 Appealing a Moderation Action

If you believe a moderation action was made in error, you have the right to appeal. All appeals are reviewed personally by the Founder.

  • Submit your appeal through the Reports section.
  • Explain what happened in your own words.
  • The Founder will review your history and the action taken.
  • You will receive a decision: accepted or denied.
  • If accepted, restrictions will be removed.

👑 Founder

The Founder has full administrative authority over Shadow Nexus Social.

Founder Permissions

  • Full access to all administrative settings.
  • Manage all users.
  • Promote or remove Administrators and Moderators.
  • Award or remove badges.
  • Suspend, restore, ban, or delete user accounts.
  • Delete or restore any post, comment, message (where permitted by platform policy), or chat.
  • Manage reports and appeals.
  • Send site-wide announcements and notifications.
  • Manage verification badges.
  • Edit site settings.
  • Enable or disable maintenance mode.
  • View analytics, logs, and audit history.
  • Manage security settings.
  • Access full database management tools.
🛡️ Administrator

Administrators assist with community moderation and have limited administrative privileges.

Administrator Permissions

  • Suspend user accounts.
  • Restore suspended accounts.
  • Review user reports.
  • Delete posts that violate the rules.
  • Delete comments that violate the rules.
  • Delete chat messages that violate the rules.
  • Send warning messages to users.
  • View moderation logs.
  • Escalate serious issues to the Founder.

Administrator Restrictions

  • Cannot promote or remove Administrators.
  • Cannot create or remove Founder accounts.
  • Cannot access Founder-only settings.
  • Cannot change global site settings.
  • Cannot access database management tools.
  • Cannot manage security settings.
  • Cannot remove the Founder.
  • Cannot change Founder permissions.
🛠️ Moderator

Moderators are responsible for maintaining a safe and respectful community environment.

Moderator Permissions

  • Remove comments that violate community rules.
  • Remove chat messages that violate community rules.
  • Review reported posts and comments.
  • Send warnings to users.
  • Report serious issues to Administrators or the Founder.

Moderator Restrictions

  • Cannot suspend or ban user accounts.
  • Cannot promote users.
  • Cannot assign or remove badges.
  • Cannot access administrator settings.
  • Cannot edit site settings.
  • Cannot view sensitive user information.
  • Cannot access Founder tools.

🤖 AI Community Safety System — Overview

The AI moderation system monitors community activity in real time and assists moderators by detecting potential rule violations. It does not replace human judgment — all serious actions require review and can be appealed.

🔍 What the AI Detects

👊 Bullying 😤 Harassment ⚠️ Threats 🔥 Hate Speech 📢 Spam 🔁 Repeated Disruptive Behavior

⚡ What the AI Can Do

  • Issue a warning when a potential rule violation is detected.
  • Track repeated violations for the same user over time.
  • Restrict chat features for users with repeated infractions.
  • Flag and escalate serious cases to moderators or the Founder for manual review.

🛡️ Safety Principles

  • The AI assists moderators — it does not operate without human oversight.
  • All significant actions include a review and appeal option for the affected user.
  • Escalated cases are always reviewed by a human before permanent action is taken.
  • The Founder has final authority over all moderation decisions and appeals.

📖 Community Guidelines

As a Moderator, uphold these community standards when reviewing content:

  1. No harassment or bullying — remove content targeting or demeaning individuals.
  2. No hate speech — remove content discriminating based on identity or background.
  3. No spam or self-promotion — hide repetitive or unsolicited promotional content.
  4. No explicit/NSFW content — remove content not suitable for all audiences unless clearly marked.
  5. No threats or violent content — always escalate to an Administrator or Founder.
  6. No doxxing or privacy violations — immediately remove and escalate to Administrator.
  7. Respect the judgment-free zone — protect users' safe space to connect.

When in doubt, escalate to an Administrator. Use warnings before mutes where appropriate.
